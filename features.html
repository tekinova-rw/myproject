How Companies Can Post Opportunities

At Job and Scholarship, we make it easy for companies to share their job openings, scholarships, and other opportunities with our global audience. Follow these simple steps to post your opportunities:

  1. Register an Account: Create a company account on our Employer Portal.
  2. Complete Your Profile: Provide details about your organization, including logo, description, and contact information.
  3. Submit Opportunity Details: Fill out our user-friendly form with details such as title, description, location, type (job, scholarship, internship, or other), application link, detailed description, requirements, responsibilities, deadline, and an optional image.
  4. Review and Publish: Our team reviews submissions for quality and relevance. Once approved, your opportunity will be live on our platform, reaching thousands of candidates.
  5. Manage Applications: Track applications and manage your postings through your employer dashboard.

Have questions? Contact us at employers@jobandscholarship.com or via WhatsApp at +250 733 253 803.
