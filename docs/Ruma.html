District SACCO – Karongi District

On behalf of our client, we are seeking to recruit a motivated professional for the position of Managing Director. The position will be based in Karongi District as detailed below.

Job Details

Job Title: Managing Director

Location: Karongi District

Reports to: Chairman of the Board

Position Type: Full-Time

Background

As part of Rwanda's long-term development plan to achieve high-income status by 2050, the country is transforming its financial sector through innovative initiatives. The Umurenge Savings and Credit Cooperatives (U-SACCOs), now consolidated into District SACCOs (D-SACCOs), are crucial in building inclusive financial systems nationwide.

U-SACCOs were created under the National Savings Mobilization Strategy and consist of 416 cooperatives serving more than 3.2 million members. After successful automation on a shared Core Banking System, the focus has shifted to consolidating them into 30 D-SACCOs to enhance efficiency and expand financial services.

Job Summary

We invite applications for the role of Managing Director. Reporting to the Chairman of the Board, the Managing Director will lead daily operations, ensure regulatory compliance, and drive strategic growth. Responsibilities include setting institutional goals, managing operational functions, preparing financial reports, developing marketing strategies, overseeing risk management, and strengthening staff capacity.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Oversee daily operations of the District SACCO and ensure regulatory compliance.
  • Set and execute growth strategies, manage SACCO development, and oversee management operations.
  • Develop plans, budgets, and initiatives as guided by the Board of Directors.
  • Create and maintain marketing and public relations strategies for SACCO products and services.
  • Prepare financial and narrative reports for stakeholders.
  • Lead staff capacity building through training and skill enhancement programs.
  • Approve internal audit plans and oversee risk management, treasury, and capital planning.
  • Drive operational improvements through strategic recommendations.
  • Ensure high-quality service delivery to SACCO members and perform duties assigned by the Board.

Qualifications & Experience Required

  • Master’s or Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Economics, Management, or Accounting.
  • Minimum 10 years of senior management experience in microfinance or banking.
  • At least 7 years of senior-level experience in finance or banking.
  • Professional certifications such as CPA, ACCA, or CIFA are highly preferred.
  • Proven leadership and relationship-building skills.
  • Excellent analytical and organizational skills; fluency in English (French is an asset).

How to Apply

Interested and qualified candidates should submit:

  • A cover letter (indicating motivation, suitability, knowledge, and experience)
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Copies of academic, professional, and training certificates
  • Copy of ID card or passport

All documents must be combined into one PDF file and sent via email to md.karongi@rumacpa.com no later than Tuesday, 25/11/2025 at 5:00 PM.

Additional Information

  • Applicants must meet all legal requirements for employment in Rwanda.
  • All information will be kept confidential according to RUMA CPA’s data privacy guidelines.
  • Only applications submitted through the email listed above will be considered.
  • Only pre-selected candidates will be contacted.
