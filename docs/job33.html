Job Description – Factory Accountant

About Us

We’re a leading Tea manufacturer seeking an experienced Factory Accountant to join our team. As a Factory Accountant, you’ll play a critical role in managing our factory’s financial operations, ensuring accurate financial reporting, and driving business growth. You will report directly to the Chief Accountant.

Position Summary

The Management of Sorwathe Ltd is seeking a dedicated and motivated Factory Accountant to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for overseeing and supporting the company’s financial operations to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and compliance. The ideal candidate will demonstrate a proactive attitude, strong analytical and communication skills, and a commitment to achieving outstanding results.

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare accurate and timely financial reports, including cost accounting, inventory management, and variance analysis.
  • Manage factory ledger accounts, ensure accurate financial data entry, and perform account reconciliations.
  • Analyze financial data to identify trends, risks, and opportunities for improvement.
  • Develop and implement financial processes and procedures to improve efficiency and accuracy.
  • Collaborate with the production team to ensure accurate costing and inventory management.
  • Ensure compliance with financial regulations, laws, and company policies.
  • Make RRA payments on time.
  • Oversee the payment process.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or a related field.
  • Professional certification (CPA, ACCA, or equivalent) preferred but not mandatory.
  • Minimum of 7 years of experience in factory accounting or a manufacturing environment.
  • Strong knowledge of accounting principles, financial reporting, and cost accounting.
  • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
  • Fluency in English is required.

What We Offer

  • Opportunities for career growth and professional development.
  • A collaborative and dynamic work environment.
  • Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance.
