Mission / Core Purpose of the Job

The Manager, Key Accounts is responsible for identifying MFS business and sales opportunities, managing partnerships with corporate and organizational clients, pitching products or services to new clients, and maintaining strong working relationships with business contacts. Additionally, the manager will drive all corporate engagements to achieve business growth.

Key Performance Areas

  • Ensure effective implementation of business development policies, procedures, tools, and techniques under guidance from the SM Fintech Business Development.
  • Build in-depth knowledge of clients’ environments, objectives, and challenges to develop tailored solutions.
  • Manage the development of business engagement plans aimed at growing the Fintech clientele base and driving revenue.
  • Oversee strategic partnerships across the Fintech ecosystem.
  • Engage corporate bodies and institutions for new business opportunities.
  • Develop contact strategies and maintain account development plans for allocated accounts.
  • Guide the team in identifying and pursuing new business opportunities.
  • Plan business sessions with prospective clients and manage their execution.
  • Contribute to increasing market share by acquiring new accounts and maintaining existing ones.
  • Ensure quality management integration in all sales activities for regional accounts.
  • Deeply understand client needs to provide best-fit solutions.
  • Provide insights to product development teams based on client feedback.
  • Support regional business development through the Field Sales Team.
  • Assist with the design and execution of promotions.
  • Implement and enforce policies, procedures, and governance mechanisms as directed by the functional lead.
  • Evaluate the efficiency and effectiveness of Corporate Accounts and propose improvements.
  • Report regularly to the SM Fintech Business Development on progress and metrics.

Experience

  • 4–6 years of relevant experience in a similar position, including at least 2 years in a managerial role.
  • Experience in Fintech, banking, or financial services is preferred.
  • Proven track record in sales, business development, or account management.
  • Experience working in a multinational enterprise with knowledge of emerging markets.

Education Background

  • Minimum of a bachelor’s degree; an MBA or master’s degree is an added advantage.
  • A 4-year degree in Business Administration, Commerce, Marketing, Economics, or a related field is preferred.
