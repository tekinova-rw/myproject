Counseling & Guidance

Serivisi zacu zifasha abantu mu rugendo rwabo rw'umwuga, imyigire, n'ubuzima bwite kugira ngo bagerwe ku ntego zabo.

1. Intego za Counseling

  • Kuyobora abantu mu gufata ibyemezo by'umwuga n'amasomo.
  • Gufasha abantu kumenya imbaraga n'intege nke zabo.
  • Kugira inama ku gucunga stress, anxiety, cyangwa ibibazo by'imibereho.

2. Ubwoko bwa Counseling dutanga

  • Career Counseling: gufasha abantu guhitamo akazi cyangwa icyerekezo cy'umwuga.
  • Academic Guidance: gufasha abanyeshuri kumenya amasomo bakwiye, preparation y'ibizamini, na study planning.
  • Personal / Life Counseling: gufasha abantu gucunga stress, umubano n'abandi, no gukemura ibibazo by'umubano.
  • Online / Remote Counseling: ushobora kuganira n'umujyanama ukoresheje internet (video / chat).

3. Uko Counseling ikorwa

  • Guhuza n'umujyanama ubifitiye uburambe.
  • Gusuzuma ibibazo cyangwa intego z'umuntu ku giti cye.
  • Kugena gahunda y'inama cyangwa sessions z'ikiganiro.
  • Kugenzura iterambere no gutanga feedback ku buryo bwo gukemura ibibazo.

4. Impamvu guhitamo serivisi zacu

  • Dufite abajyanama bafite uburambe n'ubumenyi mu by'amasomo, career, n'imibereho.
  • Serivisi zacu ziba personalized, zikumira ibibazo ku buryo bwihuse kandi bufatika.
  • Inama zitangwa mu buryo bworoshye: online, face-to-face cyangwa phone consultation.
  • Dushyira imbere confidentialité n'ubunyangamugayo mu nama zose.
