Who We Are

Job and Scholarships is a modern online platform connecting individuals with job opportunities, internships, and scholarships in Rwanda and beyond. We empower job seekers, students, and professionals to achieve their career and educational goals.

What We Offer

  • Job Listings and Applications
  • Internship Opportunities
  • Scholarship Guidance and Applications
  • Career Counseling Services
  • Resume & CV Building Support
  • Online Portfolio Hosting
  • Career Development Workshops
  • Education and Funding Consulting

Our Vision

To become a leading global platform that bridges the gap between talent and opportunity, helping people secure jobs and scholarships to unlock their full potential.

Our Mission

To empower individuals by providing accessible tools, career-building services, and scholarship opportunities in a rapidly evolving job and education landscape.
